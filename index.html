The Shinobi

Itachi Uchiha

Born into the prestigious Uchiha clan, Itachi was a prodigy unlike any before him. He awakened his Sharingan at age 8, mastered the Mangekyo Sharingan, and became a jonin at 13 — the youngest in village history.

Beneath the surface of an S-rank criminal lay a man who bore the weight of an entire village's sins. A loyal soldier who died a traitor. A brother who chose hatred over grief.

The truth of Itachi Uchiha is the most devastating story ever told — a life lived entirely in sacrifice, a death choreographed for someone else's salvation.

The Chronicle

A Life of Sacrifice

Age 8
The Sharingan Awakens

During the Third Shinobi War, Itachi witnesses his best friend Shisui's death on the battlefield, awakening his Sharingan through immense grief and trauma.

Age 11
ANBU Captain

Itachi becomes the youngest ANBU Captain in Konoha history. Simultaneously, he begins secretly reporting Uchiha coup plans to the Hokage — a brutal double life begins.

Age 13
The Night of the Massacre

Ordered by Danzo and the village elders, Itachi eliminates his entire clan in a single night to prevent civil war — sparing only his little brother Sasuke, sealing his own fate as a monster.

Age 13–21
Life in the Shadows

Joining Akatsuki as a Konoha spy, Itachi protects the village from inside the enemy organization for eight years — all while living with a terminal illness and Sasuke's hatred.

Age 21
The Final Battle

Itachi dies fighting Sasuke — deliberately losing, transferring his power, and using his last breath to poke Sasuke's forehead one final time. A villain's death. A hero's farewell.
